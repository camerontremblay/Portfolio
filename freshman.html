Freshman Year

Freshman year was a big year for me, as I had to adjust to the high school setting. However, I was up to the challenge. I passed every single class with an A and completed my first AP class. Unfortunately, I did not get involved in any clubs due to personal issues, but I still was able to make friends and enjoy myself.

Check out the classes I took below. Each class will have a description, my grade, and any other important details.

Drafting 1 Honors

Final Grade: A(100)

In Drafting 1, I learned to make basic architectural designs in the program AutoCAD. I got certified in AutoCAD as my final exam.

Healthful Living 1 Honors

Final Grade: A(96)

In Healthful Living, I learned about the teenage brain, addiction, and participated in games.

Math 3 Honors

Final Grade: A(98)

In Math 3, I learned about many types of functions, trigonometry, and geometry. I also learned how these concepts applied to the real world.

French 1

Final Grade: A(100)

In French 1, I learned "survival French". This meant I could have small conversations about things like weather, food and directions.

English 1 Honors

Final Grade: A(94)

In English 1, I studied many novels and poems and learned about character development.

French 2

Final Grade: A(97)

Unfortunately, my French 2 class was online. However, I was still able to learn more basic French including ordering at a restaurant and recreational activities.

AP World History: Modern

Final Grade: A(100)

APWH was my first AP course. I put in lots of studying to succeed, and ended up scoring a 5 on the AP exam.

Biology Honors

Final Grade: A(95)

In Biology, I learned about cells, macromolecules, ecology, and evolution.

At the end of my freshman year I realized a few things. First off, I realized that I can do well in my high school classes if I put the work in. I also realized that I needed to get involved in at least 1 club that consistently meets. Overall, I consider my freshman year a success, but wish I had done more socially.
